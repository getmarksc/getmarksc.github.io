Our story

About Get Mark

At GET MARK Professional Cleaning Services, we believe a clean space is about more than appearances. It’s about creating an environment where people can do their best work, welcome customers with confidence, and enjoy peace of mind.

After proudly serving 20 years in the United States Air Force, Mark carried the same values that guided his military career into civilian life: integrity, reliability, attention to detail, and a commitment to doing every job well.

Mark has always been the kind of person who loves to help. Whether he’s cleaning an office, organizing a workspace, or tackling a job others avoid, he takes genuine pride in doing the work well. There’s nothing he enjoys more than hearing a client say, “It looks great!” or “Thank you! We appreciate you.”

Our mission is simple: show up on time, work hard, pay attention to the details, and leave every space cleaner than we found it.

The inspiration for our business comes from a verse that has always been meaningful to Mark:

“Get Mark and bring him with you, because he is helpful to me.”

2 Timothy 4:11

Those words describe the reputation we hope to earn every day: dependable, thorough, and helpful.

Whether we’re cleaning churches, offices, professional buildings, or other spaces, our goal is the same: to serve others with excellence, integrity, and respect.

Thank you for considering GET MARK Professional Cleaning Services. We’d be honored to earn your trust and the opportunity to serve you.

Get Mark — Owner & U.S. Air Force Veteran
